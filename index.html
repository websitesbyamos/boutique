Our story

Dressed With Intention

Lophix Boutique opened on Emma Abimbola Cole Street in Lekki Phase 1 with one conviction: that what a woman wears should meet her, not compete with her. We source dresses, corporate wear, shoes, bags, and accessories that hold their shape through a full day — the meeting, the event, the drive home.

Every piece on our rail is chosen for construction first, trend second. Quality fabric, a clean finish, a fit that photographs as well as it feels. That's the Lophix standard, and it's why women return season after season.
